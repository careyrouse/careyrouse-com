Coaching FAQ

How do I get started? Apply on this page. If accepted, you will receive a private link to book a 60-minute virtual assessment.
What happens in the assessment? We will cover goals, constraints, weekly schedule, equipment and home setup, a brief movement inquiry, and whether Accountability Coaching is the right fit.
Is the assessment free? Yes.
Do you accept everyone? No. Space is limited and each application is reviewed manually.
What exactly is included in Accountability Coaching?
  • Daily accountability: a quick text or email every day confirming what you did and what is next.
  • Weekly 30-minute call: review results, remove blockers, and adjust the coming week.
  • Personalized weekly plan: simple, environment-aware programming using the equipment you actually have (gym or home) with clear "do this today" instructions.
  • Workouts you can execute: exercises with sets and reps or time targets and effort cues; cardio includes duration and intensity guidance.
  • Nutrition guidance: custom macro targets, meal frameworks, grocery and prep coaching, and weekly tweaks. You execute; I steer.
  • Use your tools: Garmin or Apple Watch, Strava, WHOOP or Oura, MyFitnessPal, and others. I read the signals you share.
  • Daily action expectation: something every day - training, mobility, or a nutrition check-in. We match my frequency, not my intensity or volume.
  • Missed days: we adjust and move. Repeated misses trigger a reset on schedule, sleep, and environment.
  • Weekly office hours: optional 10-minute virtual office hour slot you can book each week for extra questions or problem solving.
  • Monthly performance report: AI-assisted report summarizing your performance trends, consistency patterns, constraints, and recommended adjustments.
  • Achieve Anything framework app: full access to the paid version, with daily personalized email check-ins and optional monthly framework check-in conversations and access to the private Achieve Anything community group.
  • Upgrade path: after at least one month of consistent execution, I may invite you to Co-Training if fit and schedules align.
  • Price and terms: $1,000 per month, month to month. Two-week money-back guarantee on the first month.
What is the Achieve Anything framework?

The Achieve Anything framework is an 8-week operating system for turning one meaningful goal into consistent action. It closes the gap between what you say you want, what you actually do, and who you are becoming.

  • One breakthrough every 8 weeks: you choose a single 8-week breakthrough in health, money and work, or relationships.
  • Daily, concrete tasks: one small, specific action per day so you always know what to do next.
  • Simple tools for real life: routines, friction logs, and fallback plans so you adjust instead of restarting.
  • Review and repeat: weekly and end of cycle reviews so you can run the same structure again and keep raising your standards.

You can run the framework on your own or combine it with coaching. Both Accountability Coaching and Co-Training include full access to the paid version of the framework app and the private Achieve Anything community group.

What do my first 7 days look like?

Assessment is already done. Week 1 is action:

  • Day 1 plan (you still train): lock the week schedule, equipment use, and macro targets.
  • Strength: 3-5 strength sessions across the week.
  • Cardio: 0-6 days, mixed durations and intensities (some short, some longer).
  • Mobility: light mobility on about 6 days (a few minutes minimum on most days).
  • Rest: up to 1 full rest day if needed.
  • Daily check-ins: quick message confirming what you did and what is next.
  • Weekly 30-minute call: end of week review and adjustments.
How do daily check-ins work? Short text or email each day with what you completed, quick notes on wins or issues, and the plan for tomorrow. I reply with nudges or adjustments as needed.
What does the weekly 30-minute call cover? We review adherence and metrics, remove obstacles around schedule, sleep, food, and recovery, update macros and training loads, and finalize the plan for the next week.
What are weekly office hours? Once per week I open a limited office hour window. Accountability Coaching clients can book a 10 minute slot inside that window for quick questions, troubleshooting, or decision support between regular calls. It is optional and first come, first served. Co-Training support happens in person, so there is no separate office hour booking for Co-Training.
What is the monthly performance report? Once a month you receive an AI-assisted summary that pulls from your training data, check-ins, and our calls. It highlights performance trends, consistency patterns, and key constraints, then recommends specific adjustments for the next month. Both Accountability Coaching and Co-Training receive this report.
How does the Achieve Anything framework integrate with coaching? Both programs get access to the paid version of my Achieve Anything framework app. Accountability Coaching clients receive daily personalized check-in emails from the app plus optional monthly framework check-in conversations with me and access to the private Achieve Anything community group. Co-Training uses the same framework, but progress and adjustments are handled directly in our in-person sessions instead of separate automated emails, and those clients also get access to the private Achieve Anything community group.
Will you write my workouts? How specific are they? Yes. You will get exercises, sets and reps or time-based work, rest intervals when needed, and target effort using RPE, heart rate, or power. Cardio sessions include duration and intensity guidance.
Do you tell me exactly what to eat? You get custom macro targets, meal frameworks, grocery and prep guidance, and weekly tweaks. You execute. Full done-for-you meal planning is a Co-Training feature.
What apps and devices should I use? In Accountability Coaching we use whatever you already track with: Garmin or Apple, Strava, WHOOP or Oura, MyFitnessPal, and similar tools. I do not force a new ecosystem. In Co-Training, I provide the full device and subscription stack for you, including training computer, wearables, and key software subscriptions.
What if my schedule is crazy? We design around your life: shorter sessions, split sessions, and stacked days when needed. The non-negotiable is a daily touchpoint.
What if I miss a day? We adjust the week and keep momentum. If misses stack up, we reset constraints such as time of day, session length, and environment so the plan fits your life.
What if I am traveling or only have minimal equipment? I provide "minimal kit" variants using bodyweight, hotel gyms, or bands so you hit the intent of the day and keep the streak alive.
How hard will you push me? Accountability Coaching matches my frequency but not my intensity or volume. We progress intelligently - quality and consistency first, then load. Co-Training uses the same principle, with closer real-time adjustments during in-person sessions.
Injuries or medical considerations? You need medical clearance. I will modify inside safe limits, prioritize pain-free movement, and maintain progress without unnecessary risk.
Where are in-person sessions held?
  • Accountability Coaching: All Accountability Coaching training is remote.
  • Co-Training: more than 90 percent of sessions are in person. We train together at a premium gym or in a premium home gym that I purchase and have installed for you using your included equipment allowance.
Guarantee? Accountability Coaching includes a two-week money-back guarantee on the first month. Co-Training includes a two-week money-back guarantee on the first six month block.
Payments? Credit or debit card or ACH. Accountability Coaching auto-renews monthly; you can cancel any time before the next billing date. Pauses are allowed for travel or medical reasons with a return plan. Co-Training follows its six month term structure.
Privacy and data? You control what you share. I use your data to coach you and do not sell personal data.
What is Co-Training?
  • Train side by side: we are together for nearly every session.
  • Premium gym access or home gym: membership included or a premium home setup purchased and installed for you.
  • Cardio modality: personal road bike required for outdoor and indoor cycling. I provide the indoor trainer for your home setup.
  • Real-time accountability: immediate feedback and adjustments every session, plus 24/7 direct on call access.
  • Nutrition done with you: fully custom meal plan plus grocery and prep coaching.
  • Recovery included: weekly massage therapy and guided recovery sessions.
  • Testing and diagnostics: VO Max, DEXA, and full-body MRI included.
  • Devices and subscriptions: all training and tracking equipment and subscriptions (WHOOP or Oura, Garmin, Strava, MyFitnessPal, Zwift, InBody) included.
  • First month allowance: $10,000 allowance that can be applied toward a road bike purchase or premium home gym equipment.
  • Experiences: exclusive events and cycling experiences.
  • Paid Achieve Anything Framework: full access to the complete system, including the private Achieve Anything community group.
  • Terms: $20,000 per month, six month minimum, invitation only after consistent Accountability Coaching.
Who usually qualifies for Co-Training? Austin-based, able to train 5 to 6 days per week, willing to fully embrace a shared disciplined lifestyle, and ready to invest significantly. You either already ride or are ready to start riding a road bike, with equipment covered by your allowance if needed. Only one client is accepted at a time.
Do you offer speaking or team sessions? Yes. Use the contact page for speaking or team requests.
